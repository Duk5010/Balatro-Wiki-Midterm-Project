Rules of Balatro

Balatro reimagines poker by combining traditional hands with roguelike mechanics. Understanding the basic rules will help you maximize your scoring potential and progress through the game. Here's everything you need to know:

Basic Gameplay Loop

In Balatro, you progress through a series of increasingly difficult rounds called "Antes," each containing multiple "Stakes" (enemies) that you must defeat by playing poker hands. After clearing all the Stakes in an Ante, you'll enter a shop where you can spend your earned chips to buy new cards, Jokers, and other items to enhance your deck.

Playing a Hand

1. Draw 5 cards from your deck.
2. Select cards you want to discard (if any).
3. Draw replacement cards for discarded ones.
4. The game automatically identifies your best poker hand.
5. Your hand's base value is multiplied by any applicable Joker effects.
6. The total score is applied to the current Stake.

Poker Hands in Balatro

Understanding poker hand rankings is essential in Balatro. Here are the standard hands from lowest to highest value:

Card Types

Type Description
Standard Cards Regular playing cards from a standard 52-card deck.
Enhanced Cards Cards that have been upgraded to provide additional effects or values.
Sealed Cards Cards that cannot be used until unsealed by specific effects or items.
Bonus Cards Special cards that can be added to your hand without counting toward hand size limits.

Playing Cards

Balatro uses a standard 52-card deck as its foundation. Cards have suits (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs) and ranks (2-10, J, Q, K, A) that are used to form poker hands.

Playing Tip

Unlike traditional poker, in Balatro, Aces can be used as both the highest card (after King) and the lowest card (before 2) in straights. This gives you more flexibility when forming straight hands.

Game Progression

Balatro is structured as follows:

  • Blinds: The main game progression, with increasing difficulty levels (Small Blind, Big Blind, etc.)
  • Antes: Each Blind contains multiple Antes
  • Stakes: Each Ante contains multiple Stakes (enemies) you must defeat
  • Shops: Available after clearing an Ante
  • Boss Fights: Special high-difficulty Stakes at the end of each Blind

Scoring System

Your score for each hand is calculated as follows:
(Base Hand Value × Card Value Multipliers × Joker Multipliers) + Flat Bonuses

The combined score must exceed the Stake's required chip value to defeat it.

Special Cards

Balatro includes several special card types that enhance your strategy:

  • Jokers: Provide unique effects and multipliers that modify your hands
  • Tarot Cards: One-time use cards with powerful immediate effects
  • Planet Cards: One-time use cards that provide long-term benefits
  • Spectral Cards: Special versions of standard cards with enhanced effects
  • Vouchers: Special cards that provide various gameplay advantages
  • Editions: Modify your standard playing cards with various effects

Pro Tip

The key to success in Balatro is building synergies between your Jokers. Focus on combining Jokers that enhance each other's effects rather than collecting random ones.

Game Modes

Balatro offers several game modes to keep the experience fresh:

  • Standard Mode: The normal progression through Blinds
  • Eternal Mode: Endless progression with increasing difficulty
  • Daily Runs: Special challenges with the same seed for all players
  • Custom Runs: Start with specific parameters and Jokers
