Cards

Overview

In Balatro, a roguelike deck-building game, players can enhance their strategies using various card types, including the Joker Cards and Consumables such as Tarot, Spectral, and Planet Cards. Here's an overview of these card categories:

Joker Cards

Jokers are special cards that provide unique effects, bonuses, and multipliers to your poker hands. Collecting and strategically combining Jokers is key to success in Balatro.

At the start of each run, you're provided with one Common Joker. Additional Jokers can be purchased from the Shop for Chips or found in various events. You can have up to 8 active Jokers at once, with additional slots available through upgrades.

Joker Cards are unique additions to your deck that provide special abilities or effects during your run. They can be acquired directly from the shop or through Buffoon Packs. The shop typically offers up to two Joker Cards per visit, with the following rarity distribution:

  • Common Jokers: 70% chance of appearing (Basic effects, affordable)
  • Uncommon Jokers: 25% chance of appearing (Enhanced effects)
  • Rare Jokers: 5% chance of appearing (Powerful effects)
  • Legendary Jokers are exceptionally rare and can only be obtained through specific means, such as using "The Soul" Spectral Card.

Consumables: Tarot, Spectral, and Planet Cards

Consumable cards offer immediate, one-time effects that can significantly influence your gameplay. They are primarily obtained through specific Booster Packs:

  • Tarot Cards: These cards focus on altering your playing cards, such as changing their suits or ranks, and can also create new Jokers or provide additional benefits. They are obtained from Arcana Packs.
  • Spectral Cards: Offering powerful effects, Spectral Cards often come with certain costs, like destroying existing cards to gain substantial bonuses. They can be acquired through Spectral Packs.
  • Planet Cards: These cards enhance the point value of specific hands, allowing for strategic amplification of scores. They are available from Celestial Packs.

These consumable cards are stored in dedicated slots and can be utilized at strategic moments to maximize their impact during your run.


Tarot Cards

Tarot Cards are used to modify various aspects of the playing cards in the deck, such as their suit or rank. They are also capable of giving cards Enhancements, giving them additional effects, such as giving extra chips or multiplier when scored. Some of them offer cash, generate new jokers or even create more consumables.

Spectral Cards

Spectral Cards are similar to Tarot Cards in the sense that they generally modify the playing cards in the deck as well, though to a much greater extent than Tarot Cards. In exchange for these large modifications, they usually disadvantage the player in another aspect of gameplay, such as reducing hand size, destroying additional cards or consuming all money.

Ankh

Creates a copy of 1 of your Jokers at random, then destroys the others, leaving you with two identical Jokers.

Soul

Creates a Legendary Joker

Black Hole

Upgrades every poker hand by one level.
